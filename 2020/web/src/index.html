What to Know

What to eat

Unfortunately we can’t bring outside food into the rooms. The casino’s catering menu is way too expensive with a very limited selection of food options. So instead of asking everyone for more money, we'll ask for only $20 instead of $35 like last year. This will cover the room fee plus beverage and snack service. Everyone will need to buy their own lunch and dinner from a place nearby. The casino itself has many options.

What to bring

Anything but Munchkin. Casino security is on alert; you have been warned. Bring 2-4 games you want to play. The casino asked that we not bring any outside food into the rooms. They won't be too strict about drinks as long as we're tidy.

Bringing games? Add them here! If 5 other people are already bringing their copy of Kingdom Death Monster, maybe you don't need to.

What to win

Everyone will receive a free raffle ticket. If your number is called, you can choose from several wrapped boxes. Every box contains a board game. You will not be able to touch the boxes before making a selection. One of the boxes will contain an unwelcome addition to your collection. ;-)
