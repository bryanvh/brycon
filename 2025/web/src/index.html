What to eat

Feel free to bring your own grub to the venue—game fuel is a must! Prefer to venture out? Nearby Cottleville and O’Fallon offer plenty of tasty options to satisfy every craving.

Quench your thirst with complimentary soft drinks, water, and light snacks to fuel your gameplay. Feeling fancy? The cash bar is ready to level up your beverage game with a selection of grown-up potions.

No outside alcohol is allowed in the building

Gaming Principles

Be Present

Detach from your devices and give everyone your attention.

Be respectful

Congratulate those who win and encourage those who don't.

Be decisive

Calculate your next move while it's not your turn.

Be selfless

Make sure everyone else is having fun.
