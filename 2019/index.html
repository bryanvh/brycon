What to Know

What to eat

Unfortunately we can’t bring outside food into the rooms. The casino’s catering menu is way too expensive with a very limited selection of food options. So instead of asking everyone for more money, we'll ask for only $20 instead of $35 like last year. This will cover the room fee plus beverage and snack service. Everyone will need to buy their own lunch and dinner from a place nearby. The casino itself has many options.

What to bring

Anything but Munchkin. Casino security is on alert; you have been warned. Bring 2-4 games you want to play. Use this Google doc to list the games you are bringing. If 5 other people are already bringing their copy of Kingdom Death Monster, maybe you don't need to. The casino asked that we not bring any outside food into the rooms. They won't be too strict about drinks as long as we're tidy.

What to win

Raffle tickets are $1 each with no limit. We’ll start calling numbers just after dinner. If your number is called, you can choose from several wrapped boxes. Every box contains a board game. You will not be able to touch the boxes before making a selection. One of the boxes will contain an unwelcome addition to your collection. ;-)

Gaming Principles

Be Present

Detach from your devices and give everyone your attention.

Be respectful

Congradulate those who win and encourage those who don't.

Be decisive

It's not fun to watch you think.

Be selfless

Make sure everyone else is having fun.
