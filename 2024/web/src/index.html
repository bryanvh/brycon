What to Know

What to eat

Unfortunately we can’t bring outside food into the rooms. The casino’s catering menu is way too expensive with a very limited selection of food options. So instead of asking everyone for more money, we'll ask for only $20. This will cover the room fee plus beverage and snack service. Everyone will need to buy their own lunch and dinner from a place nearby. The casino itself has many options.

What to bring

Anything but Munchkin. Bring 2-4 games you want to play. The casino asked that we not bring any outside food into the rooms. They won't be too strict about drinks as long as we're tidy.

Bringing games? Add them here! If 5 other people are already bringing their copy of Frosthaven, we won't have enough room to play games, and the floor will probably collapse.

There will be prizes for the winners of Wits & Wagers.
